Lupo: his story

Lupo was found wondering around a construction site. The town rescue center was informed but sadly, they were full and couldn't take him in. They shared an urgent plea to find a foster home. He was in a dangerous place, close to a high-kill shelter and the people in the area weren't fond of his presence.

The following video was his first appearance in our lives:

We couldn't resist it, we had to take him in as soon as we watched that video. At first, he came home as a foster, but soon we knew he belonged to our family.



Some of Lupo through time

Lupo's personality evolved from the shy guy you see on the video, to an outgoing and playful rascal. These are some of his unique traits:

  • Loves chasing tennis balls
  • He is crazy about squeezy toys
  • Enjoys car drives
  • He is afraid of bigger dogs
  • He barks a lot if someone rings our home bell

Well, he is a dog after all!
